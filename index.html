Frequently asked questions

How does the shortlist process work?

We source, screen, and deliver a curated list of 3-5 pre-vetted candidates that can be reviewed anonymously.

How are candidates screened?

Our recruiters review technical fit, cultural fit, credentials, readiness, and role expectations.

Are we obligated to hire?

No. You only pay to unlock profiles you actually want to interview.

Is the intern shortlist really free for organizations?

Yes. Organizations can request intern shortlists for free and receive a shared link with full intern profile details for the selected intern shortlist.

Which industries do you support?

We support more than 40 industries, including tech, finance, healthcare, legal, creative, operations, and leadership roles.
